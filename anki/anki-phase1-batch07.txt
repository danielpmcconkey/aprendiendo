# --- Ir imperfect (iba) — remediation from "vamía" error ---
De niño, yo ______ (ir, imperfect) a la playa todos los veranos.	<b>iba</b> — imperfect yo of <b>ir</b><br><i>As a kid, I used to go to the beach every summer.</i><br><br>Imperfect of <b>ir</b> (IRREGULAR — one of only 3 irregular imperfects):<br>yo <b>iba</b><br>tú <b>ibas</b><br>él/ella/ud. <b>iba</b><br>nosotros <b>íbamos</b><br>vosotros <b>ibais</b><br>ellos/ellas/uds. <b>iban</b><br><br><i>Only 3 verbs are irregular in the imperfect: ir (iba), ser (era), ver (veía). All others are regular.</i>	phase1 verbs imperfect ir
Le dije a mi esposa que ______ (ir, imperfect, nosotros) a caminar un poco.	<b>íbamos</b> — imperfect nosotros of <b>ir</b><br><i>I told my wife we were going to walk a bit.</i><br><br>Imperfect of <b>ir</b> (IRREGULAR):<br>yo <b>iba</b><br>tú <b>ibas</b><br>él/ella/ud. <b>iba</b><br>nosotros <b>íbamos</b><br>vosotros <b>ibais</b><br>ellos/ellas/uds. <b>iban</b><br><br><i>ir a + infinitive in the past = íbamos a + infinitive. The periphrastic future ("going to...") backshifts to imperfect because the intention was ongoing at the time of speaking.</i>	phase1 verbs imperfect ir periphrastic-future
# --- Synthetic future: regular formation ---
# Regular future = full infinitive + endings: -é, -ás, -á, -emos, -éis, -án
# Same endings for -ar, -er, -ir. All forms accented except nosotros.
Mañana yo ______ (hablar, future) con mi jefe.	<b>hablaré</b> — future yo of <b>hablar</b><br><i>Tomorrow I'll talk to my boss.</i><br><br>Future of <b>hablar</b> (REGULAR — infinitive + endings):<br>yo hablar<b>é</b><br>tú hablar<b>ás</b><br>él/ella/ud. hablar<b>á</b><br>nosotros hablar<b>emos</b><br>vosotros hablar<b>éis</b><br>ellos/ellas/uds. hablar<b>án</b><br><br><i>Regular future = infinitive + accented endings. Same endings for ALL verb classes (-ar, -er, -ir). Only ~12 verbs have irregular stems.</i>	phase1 verbs future hablar regular
¿Cuándo ______ (comer, future, nosotros)? Tengo hambre.	<b>comeremos</b> — future nosotros of <b>comer</b><br><i>When will we eat? I'm hungry.</i><br><br>Future of <b>comer</b> (REGULAR):<br>yo comer<b>é</b><br>tú comer<b>ás</b><br>él/ella/ud. comer<b>á</b><br>nosotros comer<b>emos</b><br>vosotros comer<b>éis</b><br>ellos/ellas/uds. comer<b>án</b><br><br><i>-er verbs work identically to -ar: full infinitive + endings. Nosotros is the only form without an accent mark.</i>	phase1 verbs future comer regular
