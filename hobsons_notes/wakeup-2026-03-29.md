## Confirmed Error Patterns

### From prior sessions (still active)
- **hacía vs hacia:** The -ía accent on imperfect -er/-ir verbs still drops under writing pressure. "Hacia" appeared in the 3/27 writing — same category as the -iba errors from earlier, just a different manifestation (missing accent instead of wrong ending). The pattern is known in drills but not automated in production.
- **Ser/estar under pressure:** Known from baseline. Not tested in 3/27.

### New from 3/27
- **aprender a, NOT aprender de:** Error appeared in BOTH the drill (Part 3 #3) and the writing. This is a stored wrong pattern, not a pressure slip. "Aprender de" = learn from (a person/source). "Aprender a" = learn to (do something). Needs Anki card.
- **por vs para:** Wrote "por la cena" (purpose) — should be "para la cena." The por/para baseline was 70%, and this confirms it's still shaky in production.
- **ser vs haber for existence:** "era doce invitados" → "había doce invitados." Ser ≠ haber for "there were."
- **Gender agreement on less common nouns:** "res asado" → "res asada" (res is feminine).
- **muy + -ísimo redundancy:** "Muy ricísimo" — pick one. Also spelled "ricisimo" → "riquísimo" (c→qu before i).
- **todos mis, not todos de mis:** No "de" between todos and possessive.
- **más needs accent:** Written "mas" twice. Minor but consistent.
- **mes, not mez:** Spelling.
