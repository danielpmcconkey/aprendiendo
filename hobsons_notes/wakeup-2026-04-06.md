## 4/1 Homework Details

Graded this session. Two errors:

1. **Q12 (Part 2): poner/poder confusion.** Verb was poner (nosotros, preterite) — Dan wrote "pudimos" (poder) instead of "pusimos" (poner). Likely a misread, not a knowledge gap. Remediation cards in Batch 07 (poner preterite, pus- stem).

2. **Part 4 verb 6: "vamía" for ir imperfect nosotros.** Should be "íbamos." The form doesn't exist — Dan attempted to backshift "vamos a caminar" and couldn't find the right form. Remediation cards in Batch 07 (ir imperfect).

**Exercise design issue flagged by Dan:** The Part 4 narrative rewrite included "Le digo a mi esposa que vamos a caminar un poco." Dan correctly argued this sentence doesn't survive mechanical tense conversion — "vamos a caminar" is hortatory ("let's walk"), not periphrastic future ("we're going to walk"). Converting to past requires rephrasing (subjunctive: "que camináramos", or direct quote, or different verb like "debíamos"), not just backshifting. **This is a valid critique.** The exercise should not have included sentences where the pragmatic force doesn't survive conversion. Standing design rule updated: avoid hortatory/suggestion constructions in mechanical tense-conversion exercises.
