## Part 1: Read (10-15 minutes)

Look up in the Routledge: **irregular preterites** — specifically verbs with irregular stems (hic-, dij-, pud-, tuv-, estuv-, vin-, quis-, pus-, sup-, etc.).

These verbs share a pattern: irregular stem + a special set of unstressed endings (-e, -iste, -o, -imos, -isteis, -ieron). No written accents on any form. Read through the full list and note which stems surprise you.
