## Part 4: Writing — Closed Book (6 points)

Write a short paragraph (5–6 sentences) about **a specific day you remember from your trip to Granada**. Use BOTH the imperfect (for setting the scene, background, what things were like) and the preterite (for what happened, specific events) in the same narrative.

Example structure (don't copy this, just for the pattern):

- *Caminábamos por la calle cuando...* (imperfect — background)
